OUR CASHMERE

Cashmere comes from cashmere
goats undercoats,which is
extremely soft and downy. The
undercoat is protected by an
outer layer of the wool and is
made of fine,soft hairs keeps
the goats toasty during the
cold winters. It is the
undercoat that is used to make
cashmere yarn.
